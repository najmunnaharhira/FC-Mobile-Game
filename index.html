NEXT LEVEL GAMEPLAY

Take command of the pitch with new Impact Controls like Power Shot, Knock On, and Hard Tackle, that let you experience the beautiful game on mobile like never before. True Player Personality and more will bring gameplay to a new level.

LOCKER ROOM

Customize your team and players to fit your preferred style. Adjust your star players’ kit numbers and kit appearance in Locker Room, a centralised customization hub. Change the length of their socks, sleeve length, and shirt tucking style in true Locker Room fashion

VISUAL & AUDIO UPGRADES

Matchday experience is taken to a new level with new Broadcast Package updates that include match intro scenes, a new gameplay camera angle, improved goal replays, refreshed set piece cameras, and added commentary.

TRAIN YOUR ULTIMATE TEAM™

Build an Ultimate Team™ with your favorite footballers and compete in live events. Develop club legends by training your players as you craft your dream XI.

ELITE SHOOTING SYSTEM

A new revamped shooting system allows impact players to show up on the scoresheet and make their mark. Feel rewarded when you make smart shooting decisions both inside and outside the box, on a cross, or off the volley. Take control of your favourite attackers and rally your team to victory.

UEFA CHAMPIONS LEAGUE

Kick off against teams from club football’s most prestigious competitions – the UEFA Champions League, UEFA Europa League, and UEFA Europa Conference League. Take part in playable live events that correspond with the real-world tournaments throughout the football season to earn special UEFA Champions League, UEFA Europa League, and UEFA Europa Conference League players. Follow every step, from the group stages all the way to the UEFA Champions League Final.
